About Us:

Pioneering the Biomass-Based Bio-Energy Sector in India by Energising the Rural Sector through Sustainable Development Goals Punjab Renewable Energy Systems Private Limited (PRESPL) is the ‘Leading Indigenous’ Indian Entrepreneurcum-Contributor in Biomass Supply Chain Management, in India, since 2011. PRESPL has served the Farmers and the Industry with amicable Bio-Energy and Biomass Supply Chain solutions through acceptable business models; while enabling the Villagers to gain entrepreneurial acumen. PRESPL has spearheaded the Biomass Sphere by contributing to Policy Making, Technology Enhancements
