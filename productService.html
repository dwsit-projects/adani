Biomass Supply Chain Management

PRESPL excels in sustainable biomass aggregation services, sourcing and supplying high-quality agricultural residues such as paddy straw, cotton stalk, and soya husk. Our robust biomass supply chain management ensures consistent and reliable fuel delivery to manufacturing plants and industries across India, reducing dependency on fossil fuels and lowering carbon emissions. Our end-to-end supply chain solutions include collection, storage, processing, and timely delivery, helping industries reduce their carbon footprint by switching to renewable biomass fuels. PRESPLs expertise in managing large-scale biomass logistics makes us a trusted partner for companies seeking dependable biomass fuel companies across India.

Biomass Assessment & Technical Consultation

PRESPL offers specialized biomass assessment and consulting services to support the development of industrial plants and renewable energy projects. Our expert team conducts detailed resource mapping, feasibility studies, and techno-economic evaluations to help clients make informed decisions. We assist industries, policymakers, and developers in optimizing biomass utilization, improving project viability, and aligning with government renewable energy policies. PRESPLs consulting services ensure sustainable and profitable biomass energy projects across India.

Consulting Expertise:

- Biomass resource assessment and mapping
- Feasibility and techno-economic studies
- Project planning and implementation support
- Policy advisory and regulatory compliance

Biomass Briquettes and Pellets Production

PRESPL is a leading biomass briquettes and pellets supply company in India. Converting agricultural waste into high-density, eco-friendly fuel products, our biomass products are ideal for use in industrial boilers and manufacturing plants, offering a cleaner and more efficient alternative to fossil fuels. Our advanced manufacturing processes ensure consistent quality, higher calorific value, and reduced emissions, supporting clients goals for low carbon biomass energy and sustainable operations. By choosing PRESPLs biomass briquettes and pellets, industries can significantly reduce their environmental impact while optimizing fuel costs.

Product Highlights:

- High energy density biomass briquettes
- Uniform size and shape pellets for efficient combustion
- Environmentally friendly and renewable fuel source
- Suitable for power generation, pharmaceutical, alcohol, cosmetic, cement, paper, and sugar industries

Industrial Boiler Operation & Maintenance (O&M)

PRESPL provides expert biomass boiler operation and maintenance (O&M) services to ensure efficient and uninterrupted steam and power supply for industrial clients. Our experienced team manages biomass-fired boilers with a focus on safety, performance, and emission control. Through long-term O&M contracts, we help industries optimize fuel usage, reduce downtime, and comply with environmental regulations. PRESPLs O&M services are tailored to meet the specific needs of biomass power plants and manufacturing units seeking reliable renewable energy solutions.

Our O&M Services Include:

- Daily operation and monitoring of biomass boilers
- Preventive and corrective maintenance
- Emission monitoring and compliance support
- Fuel management and optimization

Build Own Operate & Transfer (BOOT) Projects

PRESPLs Build Own Operate Transfer (BOOT) projects enable industries to access renewable biomass energy infrastructure without upfront capital expenditure. We develop, operate, and maintain biomass-based power and steam plants, supplying energy as a utility service under long-term contracts. Our BOOT model includes comprehensive biomass supply chain management, ensuring consistent briquette/ pellet stock availability and plant performance. This approach supports industries in meeting sustainability targets while benefiting from cost-effective and reliable renewable energy solutions in India.

BOOT Project Features:

- Turnkey biomass power and steam plant development
- Long-term energy supply agreements (10-15 years)
- Full feedstock management and logistics
- Transfer of assets to client after contract period
