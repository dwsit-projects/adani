About Us:

Enjoying a leadership position in Biomass Supply Chain Management in India, PRESPL was established in 2011. Helmed by Indian Army Veterans, our company provides sustainable bio-energy solutions, supporting farmers, industries, and rural entrepreneurs. PRESPL's offerings include raw biomass, briquettes, and pellets, along with O&M and BOOT contracts for steam and electricity. We also manage supply chains and O&M for bio-gas, bio-CNG, bio-ethanol, and other biofuel plants.
